# 👋 Hi, I'm David Brent Panonce
## 🚀 Engineer with Purpose | Founder with Vision | Building AI for Impact

I'm a software engineer and founder based in Cebu City, Philippines, passionate about building meaningful digital products. My journey has taken me from burnout to purpose—leveraging tech to solve real-world problems through thoughtful design, AI, and code.

---

### 🧠 My Core Philosophy: Intent-Based & Guided Development

I am a strong advocate for **Intent-Based and Guided Development**. Engineering isn’t just about making things compile—it’s about deeply understanding the *why* behind the work. 

Whether I'm designing multi-agent workflows, building MCPs, or architecting cross-platform apps, my approach balances product intuition with rigorous engineering logic. I build systems that guide users toward their goals and agents that act with clear, deliberate intent. 
> *I build not just with code, but with intention. Let’s keep learning, shipping, and solving real problems.*

---

### 💡 What I Do

- 🤖 **AI Integration & Multi-Agent Systems** – Building intelligent tools and workflows using OpenAI, Vapi, LangChain, Google Gemini, and doing deep dives into **Google ADK**.  
- 🛠️ **Agent Tooling & Ecosystems** – Actively developing testing infrastructure for AI coding agents (Chalk Browser) and designing opinionated agent skills for end-to-end product development.
- 🧩 **Cross-Platform & Full-Stack Development** – Crafting seamless, scalable experiences across mobile and web platforms.
- 🏗️ **Cloud-Native Architecture** – Designing scalable, zero-downtime infrastructure using Google Cloud Platform (**GCP**), **Cloud Run**, and **Cloud Build**.
- 🌍 **Problem Solving at Scale** – Bridging tech, social impact, and real-world use cases to solve Filipino problems.
